# Screen overview

![Patient Cohort Explorer](../assets/screenshots/end-user/cohort-explorer-overview.png)

## Toolbar

The toolbar remains near the top while you scroll. It includes:

- **Home**
- font-size controls
- high-contrast and reduced-motion controls
- **Reset filters**
- filter-card layout
- display density
- theme selection

Changing display settings does not change the cohort.

## Filter sections

Cards are grouped by subject. Common sections include:

- Patient
- Cancer Type & Primary Site
- Tumor Anatomy
- Staging & Disease Extent
- Pathology & Grade
- Molecular Markers & Biomarkers
- Clinical Status
- Treatment & Interventions

Only filters present in the current dataset are displayed.

## Filter cards

Each card shows:

- the filter name;
- available values;
- a bar indicating relative frequency; and
- the patient count for each value.

After other filters are active, a count may appear as `included/total`. The first number is how many patients with that value remain in the current cohort; the second is the value’s broader total.

Select the card header to open its searchable detail dialog.

## Selected Patients drawer

The drawer appears when a filtered result is available. It can be:

- minimized to its header;
- restored;
- maximized;
- switched between the cohort table and open patient tabs.

Press **Escape** to leave the maximized state or collapse an expanded drawer.
